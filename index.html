According to data in RigPoint, as of 20 October 2020, 312 rigs have left the global fleet since the beginning of 2010 - the vast majority (80%) since 2015 around the time the effects of the previous downturn began to kick in. Attrition in the past decade peaked in 2018 when 63 units left in one year (40 jackups, 16 semis and seven drillships). This was when the vast majority of the older fleet was either retired or scrapped most notably in the North Sea standard jackup market.
